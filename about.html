Our Team

Meet the faces behind the Creative Masterpieces

From the creative artists, to the bakers and chefs and then the marketing team, we are proud to call ourselves the outstanding faces behind Aima's Delicious Pastries.

Emmanuella A. Ileogben

Chief Executive Officer

The CEO defines the bakery's long-term vision and mission, including its brand identity, market positioning, and growth objectives. She also ensures the efficient and effective operation of all aspects of the bakery, from production and quality control to sales and customer service. She oversees the procurement of ingredients and supplies, ensuring a reliable supply chain. She sets the company's culture and has a deep understanding of the bakery industry, including trends, techniques, and ingredients.

Emmanuel Aidelokhai Ileogben

Sales Manager

Our Sales Manager develops and implements sales strategies to increase revenue and market share. He builds and maintains relationships with customers, suppliers, and partners. He also analyzes sales data and market trends to identify opportunities for growth and improvement.

Divine Ekeze Favour

Head Decorative Artist

Our Head Decorative Artist designs and creates visually stunning decorations for cakes and pastries. She stays updated with the latest trends in cake decoration and pastry arts. She also trains and supervises junior decorators to maintain high-quality standards. Her artistic vision enhances the aesthetic appeal of all our products.

Emmanuel Damilola Adejube

Head Baker

The Head Baker oversees the daily production of baked goods, ensuring consistency and quality. He develops and tests new recipes to expand the bakery's offerings. He manages the baking team, providing training and guidance to maintain high standards. He ensures compliance with health and safety regulations in the kitchen. Additionally, he monitors inventory levels and coordinates with suppliers to ensure timely procurement of ingredients.
